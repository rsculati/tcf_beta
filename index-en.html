Univerre Pro Uva

1500 items in an interactive e-catalogue


Advertise the offer to create the demand


The Computer Firm completed a whole website rework, creating an A to Z tri-lingual platform, in German, French and Italian for Univerre, a major Swiss glass distributor.

We built an interactive online catalogue for them, listing over 1 500 items, connecting the site to the company’s internal stock management system, for a real-time, accurate update of stock levels. We designed the user experience to display the items so products can be browsed in the most intuitive way, naturally bringing the users to the exact item they are looking for.

Expertise


• HTML development • User Experience (UX) • Online catalogue linked to stock management system • Responsive site for mobile and tablet • Referencing (SEO)


Hypnomontreux

A website at the center of customer acquisition strategies


Convert users from site visitors into paying customers


Hypnomontreux, a hypnotherapy and natural health center, came to The Computer Firm with the objective of turning their website into an extremely efficient, highly optimised, booking tool. To achieve this, we have performed a thorough analysis of the user experience; to design it in a way that “converts” the highest number of users. In other words, it implies that people who visit the page will book an appointment and convert into paying customers, further allowing Hypnomontreux to calculate the return of investment of their website.

We have catered Hypnomontreux’s range of services clearly to bring trust and professionalism. We integrated our own, internally-developed, Content Management System (CMS). This allows our client to update the site content themselves (offers, texts, images) without depending on us. Our CMS further allows an integration of a blog component on which Hypnomontreux is able to create and post content on their own.



Memorable

Opening the doors to the best bars, restaurants and clubs in Montreal


The brainchild of The Computer Firm


The Computer Firm created the Memorable.io platform, from concept to execution. The project was introduced during a major Start-Up event in 2015.

With a passion for travelling around the world, we imagined and created Memorable in order to provide the city adventurers with the best things to do, see and eat. We developed an algorithm that pulls in real time information from carefully selected sources (highly regarded travel blogs, reputable news review platforms, lifestyle experts, etc.) and gathers them into this highly informative, curated platform.

Expertise


• HTML development, CSS, Angular & Node • Machine learning and text processing • Social Media Optimization (SMO) • Search Engine Optimization (SEO) • Search Engine Marketing (SEM) • Brand Image Management • Adwords, Google & Facebook Ads
